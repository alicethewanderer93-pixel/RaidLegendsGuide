Dungeon Strategies

Spider's Den

Difficulty: Hard

  • Use AOE damage dealers
  • Bring crowd control
  • Spiderlings target lowest HP champion
  • Recommended: Support + DD + CC

Fire Knight's Castle

Difficulty: Medium

  • Focus on shield breaks
  • Use weak hit champions
  • Speed tuning is crucial
  • Recommended: DPS + Support

Dragon's Lair

Difficulty: Very Hard

  • Poison damage is effective
  • Cleanse debuffs regularly
  • Keep team healed
  • Recommended: Healer + Poison + CC

Ice Golem's Peak

Difficulty: Hard

  • Burn damage works great
  • Attack down is valuable
  • Heal through incoming damage
  • Recommended: Support + Burn + Healer

Pro Tips & Guides

🎯 Team Building Basics

Always include: 1 Healer, 1 Damage Dealer, 1 Tank/CC, 2 Utility. This balanced approach works in most dungeons.

⚡ Speed Tuning

Speed is crucial! Aim for: Healer fastest, then DPS, then Tank. This ensures optimal turn order and survival.

💎 Resource Management

Don't waste Silver on low-tier champions. Focus on 1-2 teams first before spreading resources thin.

🏆 Gear Priority

Prioritize: Accuracy for control champs, Speed for supports, HP/DEF for tanks. Gear pieces matter more than stars.

📊 Artifact Farming

Farm dungeons 16+ for optimal rewards. Join an active Clan for bonuses. Daily quests are free resources!

🎪 Arena Strategy

Defense team: Put your fastest champion first. Offense: Speed > Burst damage. Counter meta picks for wins.
