🎯 Essential Game Tips

Early Game (Week 1-2): Focus on Campaign Cruel difficulty. Choose your starting champion (Athel, Kael, Elhain, or Galek) and get them to level 50. Don't spread resources thin—focus on one strong damage dealer first.

Gear Matters: Lifesteal gear is absolutely essential for early game. If you can farm Lifesteal sets, your champions will survive much longer. Look for pieces with good substats in Attack, Critical Rate, and Speed.

Don't Chase Gacha Early: You get 1-2 free sacred shards per month. Save them for 2x events for better value. The free champions can complete all game content.

Team Synergy: Build teams that complement each other. Have at least one damage dealer, one support, and one way to sustain (healer or lifesteal).

⚔️ Game Modes Overview

Campaign: Your primary source of experience and gear. Progress through Normal, Hard, then Brutal and Nightmare for better rewards. Use Lifesteal gear to sustain.

Dungeons: Five different dungeons (Dragon, Spider, Fire Knight, Void, Minotaur) each drop specific gear types. Dragon is easiest and best for beginners. Start with Dragon 12-13, work up to 20.

Arena: PvP content where speed and burst damage matter most. Build one strong arena team with speed buff and high damage dealers. Don't rush into competitive arena early.

Clan Boss: Weekly raid against a powerful boss. Focus on DEF down debuff and sustain. Seer is an excellent F2P champion for this.

Doom Tower: Progressive tower with increasing difficulty. Requires diverse champions. Plan your team comp for each floor. Healers are essential.

Faction Wars: Use champions from specific factions to battle others. Build secondary teams with champions from different factions.

🔗 Useful Resources

Official Game Links: Download Raid Shadow Legends on iOS or Android. Join official communities and forums.

Community Tools: Many fans have created damage calculators, team builders, and progression guides. Check community forums for recommendations.

Gear Main Stats Guide: Slot 2 is always Speed (except for supports). Slot 4 is Crit Damage or Attack %. Slot 6 depends on role (HP%, Defense%, or Accuracy).

Progression Focus: Month 1: Get to Campaign Nightmare. Month 2: Farm Dragon 20 for gear. Month 3: Add Clan Boss. Month 4+: Start Doom Tower Normal.
